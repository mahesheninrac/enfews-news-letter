Historic upgrade of the Wardha

India’s power transmission sector witnessed remarkable growth in FY 2024-25, with 133 projects commissioned and 8830 circuit kilometers of lines added — nearly double the progress seen in the previous year.

 Key Highlights:
  • Rapid expansion of 765 KV transmission lines, soaring from 918 ckm in Oct '24 to 2158 ckm by Mar '25.
  • Historic upgrade of the Wardha-Sambhaji nagar line from 400 KV to 1200 KV, enabling 6000–8000 MW transfer capacity – a strategic win for PSP corridors.
  • PGCIL leads with over 2500 ckm of new lines, while state utilities top capacity additions overall.

  • surge in 220 KV line additions in feb 25 adding nearly 2958 ckm to the grid

HYDGEN Innovation Day 2025 Pioneering India's Green Hydrogen Future

India Launches Hydrogen Powered Bus and Truck Trials to Boost Green Mobility Under National Mission

HYDGEN Innovation Day 2025 was held at Sahyadri Campus, focusing on the future of green hydrogen in India. The event brought together policymakers, scientists, industry experts, and academicians to promote collaboration and innovation in clean energy. Karnataka's government announced its commitment to green hydrogen initiatives.

The Indian government has launched five pilot projects under the National Green Hydrogen Mission to deploy hydrogen-powered buses and trucks. These projects involve 37 vehicles, including hydrogen fuel cell and internal combustion engine-based models, along with nine hydrogen refueling stations.

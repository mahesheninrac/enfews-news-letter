Essar to Invest ₹30,000 Crore in Green Hydrogen Plant in Gujarat

Essar Group plans a ₹30,000 crore investment in a green hydrogen plant in Jamnagar, Gujarat, as part of its clean energy strategy. Prashant Ruia, Essar Capital director, aims to decarbonise a UK refinery, build a green steel plant in Saudi Arabia.

Analyst Insight
The Essar Group is making a substantial investment in a green hydrogen plant located in Jamnagar, Gujarat. Gujarat stands at the forefront of India's clean energy transition, aiming to produce 60% of the nation’s green hydrogen by 2030. Leveraging its leadership in key industries such as steel, refining, and chemicals, Gujarat is well-equipped not only to drive decarbonization efforts across these sectors but also to meet the increasing demand for green hydrogen. → Read Full Insight

HydrogenPro Set to Boost Gen3 Electrode Production Capacity

Canada Enacts 40% Tax Rebate for Clean Hydrogen Investments

HydrogenPro is set to invest in a new production line for its Gen3 electrodes, aiming for an annual capacity of at least 90-100MW. The Gen3 technology enhances electrolysis efficiency, lowering the levelised cost of hydrogen (LCOH).

Canada's clean hydrogen investment tax credit, offering producers 15% to 40% tax rebates on "eligible equipment" for H2 projects, is now law. It was included in the C-69 bill, passed by the House of Commons and received royal assent from Governor-General Mary Simon.

South Korea Plans First Nuclear Hydrogen Project by 2027 with Hyundai, Samsung

Genevos Selected for Lithuania's First Hydrogen-Electric Ship Fuel Cell System

South Korea plans its first nuclear hydrogen production plant by 2027, with Hyundai Engineering & Construction, Samsung C&T, Doosan Energy, and Korea Hydro & Nuclear Power (KHNP) among the eight companies signing a cooperation agreement.

At the Port of Klaipeda, Baltic Workboats and Western Baltija Shipbuilding are building a 42m x 10m zero-emissions waste collector. Genevos will supply the H2-Pack with two HPM-40 units as range extenders, boosting the vessel's 2,000 kWh battery system by 40% at 8 knots.

FRV Announces $5 Billion Investment in 2 GW Green Hydrogen Capacity in Brazil

Madrid-based FRV, part of Jameel Energy, plans a 2 GW green hydrogen project at Brazil's Pecém Complex in Ceará, focusing on green ammonia for export to Europe and Asia. With a BRL 27 billion ($5 billion) investment, it aims to create 1,500 construction jobs and 200 operational jobs.

Analyst Insight
Brazil has become a big market player in the green hydrogen sector due to a big $5 Billion Investment in 2 GW Hydrogen Capacity announced by FRV,part of Jameel Energy. Vertiv, Ballard Power Team for Hydrogen Data Center Backup

Silveline Power Teams Up with SRAM Group for Hydrogen Vehicle Development

Vertiv & Ballard Power Systems team up for hydrogen-powered backup solutions for data centers. Vertiv integrates Ballard's 200kW fuel cell modules with their Liebert® EXL S1 UPS in Ohio, combining with cooling systems, power conditioning, hydrogen storage.

Silveline Power signed a USD 135 million licensing deal with SRAM & MRAM Group for hydrogen fuel-based electric vehicle technology. The agreement focuses on developing EV technologies and ancillary supply chains over five years.

ExxonMobil Teams with Air Liquide in Top Low-Carbon Hydrogen Project

ECL Launches First Hydrogen-Powered Green Data Center

ExxonMobil and Air Liquide partner in Baytown, Texas for low-carbon hydrogen and ammonia. Air Liquide transports hydrogen via existing pipelines and installs four Large Modular Air separation units using low-carbon electricity.

ECL, a Silicon Valley DCaaS firm, debuts a modular, off-grid data centre delivering 1MW per block, customizable for users. Their hydrogen-powered facility now serves Cato Digital, offering sustainable bare metal cloud services.

MNRE Updates Guidelines for Green Ammonia Production Procurement

MNRE aims for five million tons of annual green hydrogen production by 2030. Released on January 16, 2024, the SIGHT program guidelines focus on Mode 2A: Incentive for Green Ammonia Production, catering initially to 550,000 tons yearly for the fertilizer sector.

Analyst Insight
Ministry of New and Renewable Energy (MNRE) is aiming to achieve a production capacity of 5 million tonnes per annum of Green Hydrogen by 2030. IISER Tirupati Develops Method for Hydrogen from Methanol and Formaldehyde

Partners Seal Deal for BarMar Hydrogen Infrastructure Development

Researchers developed a method using methanol and paraformaldehyde to efficiently generate hydrogen, advancing sustainable energy amid fossil fuel depletion. These substances serve as effective carriers due to their abundance and manufacturability.

Enagás, GRTgaz, Teréga, and OGE signed a JDA on June 25 in Madrid for the BarMar project, focusing on feasibility, FID conditions, and SPV governance. Enagás owns 50% in Spanish and French sections, GRTgaz holds 33.3%.

$34 Billion Green Hydrogen Project in Mauritania Spotlights Africa's FDI Surge

Hygenco and Ameropa Forge Green Ammonia Supply Pact, Targeting 1.1 MTPA by 2030

In 2023, Africa's FDI flows fell 3% to $53B, driven by mixed trends in Egypt and South Africa. Despite this, the continent saw a rise in global greenfield megaprojects, notably in Mauritania.

Hygenco Green Energies partners with Ameropa to supply green ammonia from its Gopalpur port, Odisha plant starting 2027, producing 600 TPD initially, scaling to 1,200 MTPD by 2028 and full capacity of 1.1 MTPA by 2030.

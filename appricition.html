🚀 A Proud Moment for Eninrac Consulting! 🌍⚡

We are honored to receive an Appreciation Letter from the Economic Research Institute for ASEAN and East Asia (ERIA) for our contribution to their flagship project, "Electric Vehicles in ASEAN: Total Cost of Ownership, Externalities, and Sustainable Strategies."

📌 Our team, led by Mr. Ravi Shekhar & Ms. Nitika Sharma, played a crucial role in developing analytical key insights and policy recommendations for the chapter "Power and Charging Infrastructure Investment Requirement for Adopting EV in ASEAN."

📊 This recognition reinforces our commitment to delivering data-driven insights that support sustainable energy transitions and infrastructure advancements in the ASEAN region.

💡 At Eninrac, we continue to drive impactful research, collaborate with global leaders, and shape policies for a greener future.

Odisha’s wind power potential estimated at 12,129 MW

The National Institute of Wind Power (NIWE) has estimated that Odisha has a significant wind power potential of 12,129 MW at 150 meters above ground level. Potential sites for wind projects have been identified in nine districts across the state. Odisha doesn’t have any grid connected wind power projects as of date...

Analyst Insight
Being a coastal state, Odisha receives considerable amount of wind throughout the year. High average wind speeds of the state make it a suitable prospect for harnessing wind energy. However, Odisha barely has any wind energy infrastructure as of date.

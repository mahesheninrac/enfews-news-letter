The Union Budget for 2025-26 allocated Rs 500 crore to the Wind Energy sector, a 37.5% decrease from the previous year. This reduction is linked to a reduced need for generation-based incentives for older projects. Senvion India and Voodin Blade Technology Collaborate on Sustainable Wind Blades

POWERGRID Invests ₹429.16 Crore in 500 MW Wind Energy Projects and Transmission Projects in Gujarat

Senvion India has partnered with Germany's Voodin Blade Technology to develop Wooden Wind Turbine Blades for its 4.2 MW platform. The collaboration will begin with a feasibility study, followed by prototype installation and testing. Voodin’s sustainable, wood-based blades, made from Laminated Veneer Lumber (LVL), address recyclability and manufacturing energy efficiency...

The first involves a 400kV switchyard at Vataman to support 500 MW of Offshore Wind capacity off Gujarat’s coast, with a cost of ₹319.42 crore and a 2026 commissioning date. The second project focuses on improving transmission to the Dholera area...

SJVN's Tender Results Announce for 600 MW Wind Project at Tariff ₹3.98-3.99/kWh

Germany’s Wind Shortfall Increases Fossil Fuel Reliance

SJVN has awarded Adyant Enersol and UPC Renewables the winners of its 600 MW Wind Energy Project. The two companies will develop ISTS-connected Wind Power Projects on a build-own-operate basis. Adyant Enersol secured 70 MW at ₹3.98/kWh, while UPC Renewables won 100 MW at ₹3.99/kWh...

Germany’s Wind Power output dropped 3% in 2024, with a 22% decline in the last quarter due to lower wind speeds. As a result, Germany increased power imports and fossil fuel use, leading to higher regional power prices across Europe. In January 2025, wholesale prices surged 47% compared to the previous year...

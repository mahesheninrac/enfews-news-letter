Equinor and Oceanex Energy Granted Wind Farm Feasibility License in Australia

Mississippi's Delta Wind: US's Tallest Turbines, 184.5 MW

The Australian government granted Equinor and Oceanex Energy a feasibility license to explore a wind farm off Newcastle's coast, potentially generating over 2 GW of electricity. If successful, Oceanex could pursue a commercial license under new offshore wind development laws.

Mississippi launched its first utility-scale wind farm, Delta Wind, featuring the tallest onshore wind turbines in the US. Located in Tunica County near Arkansas and Tennessee, it's owned by AES Corporation.

Labour Ends Onshore Wind Ban to Boost Security in England

Vestas Wins EPC for 577 MW Golden Plains Wind Farm Stage 2 in Australia

At RenewableUK's Global Offshore Wind conference, Ed Miliband announced Labour's plan to end the nine-year onshore wind ban in England. The policy, praised by industry leaders, aims to boost energy security and unlock billions in investments.

Vestas strengthens partnership with TagEnergy, securing EPC for Golden Plains Wind Farm Stage 2 in Australia. Featuring 93 V162-6.2 MW turbines, the extension adds 577 MW to the existing 756 MW, totaling 1.3 GW—the largest onshore wind farm by Vestas.

Middle East and North Africa: Potential for 1,400GW Offshore Wind

An analysis by the Global Wind Energy Council reveals the Middle East and North Africa could harness 1,400 GW of offshore wind energy. Saudi Arabia, Morocco, Egypt, and Oman are poised to lead development, aiming to diversify energy sources.

Analyst Insight
Wind power is increasingly pivotal in global sustainable energy strategies, driven by advancements in technological that reduce costs and expand infrastructure. The MENA region, like Saudi Arabia, Morocco, Egypt, and Oman, features expansive coastlines and favorable wind conditions, ideal for developing offshore wind projects. Orsted, Eversource's 942MW Sunrise Wind Gets BOEM Approval

PUCSL Denies Approval for AGESL's 484 MW Wind Project

Orsted and Eversource's Sunrise Wind project, a 942MW offshore wind farm off New York, secured final approval for its Construction and Operations Plan from BOEM. The project will power nearly 600,000 homes, featuring one nautical mile turbine spacing and environmental protections.

The Public Utilities Commission of Sri Lanka (PUCSL) declined approval for Adani Green Energy SL Ltd.'s 484 MW wind power project in Mannar and Pooneryn due to inadequate information from the Ceylon Electricity Board (CEB).

TotalEnergies, EnBW Win $3.2bn Auction for 2.5GW Wind Farms

JSW Steel USA Plans $110 Million Baytown for offshore wind Upgrade

TotalEnergies and EnBW have won a $3.2bn auction by the German Federal Network Agency for offshore wind sites. They secured rights to develop 2.5GW capacity, including EnBW's 1GW North Sea wind farm, operational by 2031.

JSW Steel USA plans a $110 million investment in Baytown, Texas, to modernize its steel plate mill with sustainable technology. This initiative aims to produce high-quality steel plates supporting US offshore wind expansion goals, potentially powering 10 million homes.

NCT to Discuss Rs. 18,371 Crore Offshore Wind Transmission Plans

The NCT plans to discuss mega transmission schemes for offshore wind farms in Gujarat & Tamil Nadu, each having 30 GW potential. Projects aim to commission a 500 MW plant in Gujarat by March 2028 and a 500 MW in Tamil Nadu by March 2029.

Analyst Insight
NCT Plans Rs. 18,371 Crore for Offshore Wind Power Transmission and Viability Gap Funding (VGF) scheme helped in this venture. The VGF support from the Government will reduce the cost of power from offshore wind projects and make them viable for purchase by discoms. GE Vernova: 88 Wind Turbines, 248 MW Montana Project

Major Step: DTEK Plans Ukraine's Largest 650MW Wind Farm

GE Vernova Inc. has begun delivering 88 of its 2.8 MW-127m wind turbines for Puget Sound Energy’s Beaver Creek wind farm in Montana. Scheduled for completion in 2024, the project will generate 248 MW, powering 83,000 homes annually and creating 150-200 construction jobs.

DTEK's plan for a 650MW wind farm, Ukraine's largest, gains traction with grid connection specs secured. Construction slated for next year in Poltava region aims to bolster national capacity amid energy security concerns, post-war setbacks.

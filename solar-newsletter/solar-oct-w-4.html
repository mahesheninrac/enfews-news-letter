CERC's recent amendments to the Indian Electricity Grid Code enhance grid operation by setting limits on infirm power injection and refining scheduling rules. Key changes include a one-year limit for conventional power and a 45-day limit for Renewables. Additionally, compensation structures for part-load operations are clarified...

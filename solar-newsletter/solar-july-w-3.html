PM Surya Ghar Scheme: KERC's Subsidized Solar

KERC's latest order proposes FY25 tariffs for Karnataka's grid-connected solar plants. MW-scale projects set at Rs3.04/unit, with BESS at Rs5.66/unit. Rooftop solar tariffs vary: 1 KW up to sanctioned load at Rs .20/unit.

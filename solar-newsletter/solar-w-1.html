Shakti Pumps Wins 558 cr Contract for Solar Water Pumps in Uttar Pradesh

Shakti Pumps (India) Ltd has received a Letter of Intent from Uttar Pradesh's Department of Agriculture for 12,537 solar water pumps under PM-KUSUM Phase-III, Component-B. The contract, valued at INR 558.16 crore ($66.65 million)...

Analyst Insight
The letter of intent for installation of standalone solar agriculture pumps by Uttar Pradesh's Department of Agriculture shows governments intent for faster execution and adoption of PM-KUSUM scheme. This will help in replacing of existing diesel agriculture pumps/irrigation systems in off-grid areas, where grid supply is not available. The faster installation will help the farmers of UP achieve water security through the provision of assured water sources through solar water pumps. Solar energy powered pumps will provide a reliable and eco-friendly solution for farmers reducing their electricity utility bills. Oorja Raises $1.5 Million for Solar Expansion in India

Oorja, a Solar Irrigation and Advisory Service for smallholder Farmers in India, secured $1.5million in Pre-Series A funding. The round includes $1.2M from impact investors like Acumen & $300,000 from the Swiss Re Foundation. Oorja plans to expand its Solar infrastructure and B2B2C services...

Analyst Insight
Oorja has recently secured $1.5 million in Pre-Series A funding, comprising both equity investments and a grant. This capital will be directed towards expanding its Agri-energy infrastructure and enhancing its impact on agriculture. The funds will facilitate the deployment of solar-powered irrigation and milling systems, replacing diesel-powered pumps and irrigation systems in off-grid regions where grid electricity is unavailable. The introduction of solar-powered pumps offers a sustainable and environmentally friendly alternative for farmers. Oorja also plans to implement a Business-to-Business-to-Consumer (B2B2C) model to deliver solar irrigation solutions to agribusinesses and SMEs.

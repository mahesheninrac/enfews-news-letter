Electric Vehicles Market Development In Australia - Fact Sheet

 

We’re excited to share our latest Insight by Horizon, A Eninrac Consulting Company blog post, post: Australia's EV Market – Surge in 2024, Slow Start to 2025 Our newest blog explores how Australia’s EV market experienced significant growth in 2024, with over 114,000 EVs sold, marking a 17% year-on-year rise. However, 2025 has opened on a slower note, spotlighting the need for stronger momentum and strategic shifts to meet climate targets.

 

With more than 1,000 fast and ultra-fast charging stations, and ambitious federal and state policies including the National EV Strategy and Vehicle Efficiency Standards, Australia is laying the groundwork for a cleaner mobility future.

 

However, the rise of hybrid vehicles and supply-side challenges highlight the urgent need for stronger government action and robust EV infrastructure to stay on course for 1 million EVs by 2027.

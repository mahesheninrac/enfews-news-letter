GERC Approves GETCO's Land Use for BESS Projects

GERC approved GETCO's petition to use surplus land at substations for Battery Energy Storage System (BESS) Projects. This decision aims to enhance energy reliability by storing Renewable Energy for peak demand. The BESS projects will not interfere with GETCO’s transmission operations...

Integration of large amount of renewable energy into the grid presents the difficulty of maintaining grid supply and providing uninterrupted power supply and hence Indian government is planning to invite bids for grid scale ESS projects to bring down the variability in RE supply. Indigenous capability for manufacturing of BESS compatible with advanced chemistry cells will help meet the demand of domestic market which will result in decrease of import of IC engine vehicles and promote locally manufactured EVs and hybrid vehicles. India issues 10GW energy storage tender: Joint Secretary Vijay Mittal

CIP Invests in 220MW/1100MW Battery Storage Project in Chile

The Ministry of Heavy Industries is set to release a 10 GW RFP, it will be issued in collaboration with the Ministry of New and Renewable Energy. In summit 300 leaders participated discuss issues like R&D, manufacturing, supply chain, and recycling of lithium-ion batteries. ACC-PLI scheme is expected to significantly boost battery manufacturing capacity with over 40GW/hr out of a targeted 50 GW.

Copenhagen Infrastructure Partners (CIP) has announced a final investment decision for a 220-MW/1,100-MWh Battery Energy Storage System in northern Chile, its first in the country. Named Arena BESS, construction will begin in early 2025, aiming for operational status by early 2026. The project will enhance solar integration and support Chile's renewable energy goals.

EIG Launches Fidra Energy for 10 GW Battery Storage Development

Torrent Power Secures 2000 MW Energy Storage Contract in Maharashtra

EIG has launched Fidra Energy, a European battery storage platform based in Edinburgh, targeting a 10 GW capacity by 2030. The flagship project, Thorpe Marsh, will be Europe’s largest battery site at 1.45 GW, capable of powering 800,000 homes. Additional projects include Bicker Fen and West Burton, together meeting over 5% of the UK's peak electricity demand.

Torrent Power Ltd has secured a long-term contract from MSEDCL to supply 2,000 MW of Energy Storage from its Pumped Hydro Storage Plant in Maharashtra. This agreement, extending over 40 years, enhances the previously confirmed capacity of 1,500 MW by an additional 500 MW. The initiative aims to meet rising Energy Storage needs alongside Renewable Energy integration.

Power Sector Regulators Key to Battery Storage Market Expansion

India's Power Sector regulators are crucial for unlocking trillions in Battery Energy Storage investments, essential for a reliable grid. Following record demand and infrastructure strain during the 2024 heatwave, Battery Storage Systems (BESS) present a viable solution. However, to attract investment, regulators must recognize and monetize the full range of BESS services beyond energy savings.

JSW Neo Secures 1500 MW/12000 MWh PHES with Battery Storage in Maharashtra

GIGA Storage Unveils Ambitious 700 MW/2800 MWh BESS in Belgium

JSW Neo Energy Limited, a subsidiary of JSW Energy, secured a Letter of Intent for 1,500 MW / 12,000 MWh of Pumped Hydro Energy Storage from MSEDCL for its Bhavali project in Maharashtra. This boosts its total Energy Storage capacity to 16.2 GWh, comprising 14.4 GWh of Pumped Hydro and 1.8 GWh of Battery Storage. The company aims for 40 GWh by 2030, enhancing grid stability and RE integration.

GIGA Storage Belgium is set to build the Green Turtle battery park in Dilsen-Stokkem, designed to be the largest battery energy storage system in continental Europe, with a capacity of 700 MW/2,800 MWh. Strategically located near a high-voltage substation, it will enhance Belgium's energy transition by optimizing renewable energy use. Construction begins in 2025, targeting completion by 2028.

NGEAL Invites Bids for 25MW/100MWh Battery Storage Project in Assam

Synergy’s first units Installed 500MW/2000MWh BESS in Western Australia

The New and Green Energy of Assam (NGEAL) invites bids for a 25MW/100MWh Battery Energy Storage System Project at the 400 kV Kurkurmara Grid Substation at Mirza Kamrup, District Assam, due by November 25, 2024. Bidders require ₹10 million security, ₹23,600 for documents, and must demonstrate relevant experience. Project completion is within 12 months, with O&M required for 12 years.

The first 80 units of the 500MW/2,000MWh Collie battery energy storage system in Western Australia have been installed by state-owned Synergy, with completion expected in 2025. Funded by AU$2.3 billion from the state budget, the project aims to support renewable energy and manage peak loads. Premier Roger Cook highlighted its significance for clean, reliable energy.

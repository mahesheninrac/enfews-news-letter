India's First Metro BESS: Kolkata's Milestone Achievement

Lion and Giga Storage Expand Netherlands 300MW+ BESS Capacities

India's Kolkata Metro is set to install its first BESS on the Blue Line, a pioneering move in the country's metro networks. The system, ordered from M/s Delta Electronics Pvt. Ltd., features a 4 MW BESS with 6.4 MWh capacity using Lithium Iron Phosphate batteries.

Lion Storage secures permit for a 347MW/1,457MWh BESS in Vlissingen, aiming for 2026 operation. Meanwhile, Giga Storage plans a 300MW/1,200MWh project in northern Netherlands this year. Both developments signal significant progress for Dutch grid-scale energy storage.

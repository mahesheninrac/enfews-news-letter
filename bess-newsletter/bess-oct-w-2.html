Form Energy Raises $405 Million for Battery Development

California Sets New Records for Battery Energy Storage

Form Energy, a Massachusetts-based company specializing in iron-air batteries, has raised $405 million in a Series F funding round led by T. Rowe Price. This capital will help develop its battery capable of storing electricity for 100 hours, crucial for enhancing Renewable Energy use and grid reliability...

As of October 2024, CAISO has connected 10.219 GW of utility-scale Solar to California’s grid, marking a 0.9 GW increase from August. Battery Storage records were also set, with a peak discharge of 5.22 GW in September. California now has 177 GW of Energy Storage planned through 2030...
